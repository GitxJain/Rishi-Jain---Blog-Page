My JEE journey

For those living under a rock , JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) , is one of the toughest competitive exams of India , which comprises of three subjects , Physics , Chemistry , Maths. It's a 3 hour , MCQ format examination , which tests your fundamentals of science and gauges how well you are able to apply them in various real life problems. Students prepare for this one exam , for serious durations of time , ranging from 2 to 5 years ! If this doesn't tell you the complexity of the exam , I dont know what will. So my 11th standard began , and so did my JEE prep. Initially , I was unable to digest the pattern of learning. In school , all we did was learn and spill in our exams. It was quick and easy and things were dealt with good pace , without any second thought. Here , when I saw that each concept takes up a week to swallow , I couldn't handle it . This lead me to have serious depression in the first seven months of my JEE preparation , and a lot of other personal problems added to my troubles. Then , in the 12th grade , it was go hard or go home , and I ain't tryna go home . So , I went crazy with my preparation , studying 6-7 hours a day , solving past year papers , reading world class textbooks to understand the fundamentals. Lastly , after various ups and downs , I gave my JEE Mains and Advanced , and cleared both. I gave CET as a backup exam , and turns out this only helped me secure admission into DJ Sanghavi College of Engineering. These 2 years have taught me a lot about discipline , human relationships , will power etc. I highly recommend someone pursuing science to live these 2 years of JEE , it was absolutely worth it!
